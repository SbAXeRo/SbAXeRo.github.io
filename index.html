After taking the AP and Honors classes on 2D and 3D animation, I decided to continue my discovery and journey by founding a club centered around 3D modelling and 3D animation. Initially, I was going to make the club into something of a large group project, as I had recently been watching 3D CGI Meetup projects on YouTube. However, to tailor the club objectives to the availability and background of each of the club members, most of whom were super busy 11th graders, I had reduced the scope of the project and reassigned roles and responsibilities more appropriate to each member. In the end, we all enjoyed the camaraderie and the learnings we all had from the club.

  • Created an animation project and assigned each member with fitted tasks
  • Taught club members how to use and animate 3D models
  • Led weekly meetings and planned club events
